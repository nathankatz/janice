OUR MISSION

"I recognized the need for Waterman Hurst the first time I decided to change jobs. Since I was responsible for the integration of the two companies, I did not have time to conduct a strategic job search, and since I had never looked for a job beyond a cashier role in college, I had no idea how to conduct a search at any level, let alone the C level.

The career counselors and coaches that worked privately did not have the expertise, networks, nor proven integrity I needed. Given my education and strong interest in careers and career development, as well as my expertise in leadership development and acquisition, I knew that some day, I would start a high level private career services company.

When I founded Waterman Hurst, I had 12 years as a corporate officer under my belt and had made three company to company moves, changing industries and moving into a #2 operating role within a public company.
